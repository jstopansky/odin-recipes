Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Water
  • Sausage
  • Italian Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta to water. Cook for 10 minutes
  3. Place pasta in pan
  4. Add sausage and cheese to pan with pasta
  5. Bake in oven for 20 minutes at 400 F
  6. Remove from oven. Add more cheese on top and let rest for 5 minutes. Serve with salad and garlic bread.
