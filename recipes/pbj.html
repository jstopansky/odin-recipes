Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on a second slice of bread
  3. Place the peanut-butter-covered slice of bread on top of the jelly-covered slice of bread with the peanut butter contacting the jelly
  4. Cut in half diagonally
