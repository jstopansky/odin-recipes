Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Ketchup
  • Yello Mustard
  • Barbeque Sauce
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Add beef to mixing bowl with salt and pepper
  2. Mix in ketchup and mustard
  3. Add egg to meat mixture
  4. Mix on medium setting for 3 minutes
  5. Sprinkle in bread crumbs
  6. Form into loaf and bake in pan for 25 minutes at 375 F
  7. Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes
  8. Cover with barbeque sauce
